Recently, Moongate secured an investment from Richard Wang, partner of DFJ Dragon Fund. Richard Wang has over 18 years’ experience of investment and technical marketing in high technology area. Prior to DFJ, he invested in several representative high-tech projects, among which Vechain ranks Top 20 in cryptocurrency area with its outstanding market value.

According to recent research, global capital funds are offering their trust to cryptocurrency mining, “Cryptocurrency mining is a targeting area with huge potential and high return.”, commented Richard Wang.

As a collective cryptocurrency mining asset token, Hashrate Token, the prior product of Moongate, takes an innovative step forward for the whole crypto finance industry, and it’s expected to become the grayscale of cryptocurrency mining.

DFJ Dragon Fund, a prominent international blockchain fund founded by Tim Draper, the Always-On #1 top venture capital deal maker. His successes in cryptocurrency area include several well-known projects, such as Telegram, Ledger, Certik, and Oasislab, etc.
